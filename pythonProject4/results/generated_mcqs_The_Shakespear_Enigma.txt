## MCQ 1
Question: What were the fruits of the Shakespear tree described as?
A) Golden apples
B) Shimmering pears
C) Glowing plums
D) Silver berries
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: What did Emma dream of becoming?
A) A painter
B) A singer
C) A writer
D) A dancer
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question: What happened after Emma ate the Shakespear fruit?
A) She forgot her dreams
B) She saw visions of her future
C) She became ill
D) She lost her ability to write
Correct Answer: B
